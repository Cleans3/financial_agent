Techcombank (TCB) is a leading Vietnamese private commercial bank known for innovation in digital banking and fintech. The bank reported FY2023 net profit of 4.3 trillion VND with return on equity (ROE) of 18.2%. TCB's digital banking platform serves over 3 million users. The bank is aggressively expanding its credit portfolio with focus on consumer lending and SME financing. Asset quality remains strong with NPL ratio below 1%. TCB has grown market share steadily, with deposits increasing 15% year-over-year. Recent analyst consensus suggests positive outlook for continued growth. Current trading price: 18,500-19,200 VND per share.
