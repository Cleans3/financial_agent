Vinamilk (VNM) is Vietnam's largest dairy company and a major player in the food and beverage sector. The company operates across dairy, beverage, and nutritional products with a dominant market share in Vietnam. Vinamilk exports to over 100 countries worldwide. In 2024, the company is focusing on premium product lines and international expansion. The company has reported consistent dividend payments, with an average dividend yield of 3.2% in recent years. Key financial metrics for FY2023: Revenue of 55 trillion VND, net profit of 5.8 trillion VND. Recent developments include expansion into plant-based products and continued investment in supply chain modernization.
