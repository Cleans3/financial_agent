FPT Corporation (FPT) is a leading Vietnamese technology conglomerate with diversified operations in telecommunications, IT services, and media. In Q3 2024, FPT reported revenue of 5.2 trillion VND, representing a 12% year-over-year increase. Net profit reached 650 billion VND, up 8% from the same period last year. The company's telecommunications segment, which accounts for 45% of total revenue, saw strong subscriber growth. FPT's IT services division benefited from increased digital transformation demand across Southeast Asia. Current stock price: 89,500 VND per share. Market capitalization: approximately 360 billion USD equivalent.
