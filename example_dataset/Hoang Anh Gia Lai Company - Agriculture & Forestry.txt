Hoang Anh Gia Lai (HRC) operates in agriculture, forestry, and rubber production with significant landholdings across Vietnam and Cambodia. The company manages approximately 1 million hectares of forestry and agricultural assets. HRC's revenue is primarily driven by rubber production, timber, and agricultural commodities. The company faces market volatility due to commodity price fluctuations. In 2023, HRC reported revenue of 2.3 trillion VND with an operating margin of 8%. The company is investing in sustainable forestry practices and exploring renewable energy opportunities on its land. Recent stock volatility: trading range of 6,000-9,000 VND per share in 2024.
