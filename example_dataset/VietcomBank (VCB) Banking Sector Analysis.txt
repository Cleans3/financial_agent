VietcomBank (VCB) is one of Vietnam's "Big Four" state-owned commercial banks and the largest by assets. As of 2024, VietcomBank maintains total assets exceeding 2.1 quadrillion VND. The bank's core business includes retail banking, corporate lending, and investment banking services. Recent performance shows a net interest margin of 3.8%, with non-performing loan ratio maintained at approximately 1.2%, well below sector average. The bank pays regular dividends, with recent payout ratios around 40% of net profit. VietcomBank is actively modernizing its digital banking infrastructure and expanding fintech capabilities. Stock price range in 2024: 33,000-38,000 VND per share.
