PetroVietnam Gas (GAS) is a major Vietnamese energy company specializing in liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) distribution. The company operates over 7,000 retail outlets across Vietnam and holds a dominant market share in the LPG sector. GAS reported Q2 2024 revenue of 4.8 trillion VND with net profit of 280 billion VND. The company benefits from stable energy demand and its extensive distribution network. GAS pays regular quarterly dividends with historical yield around 2.5-3%. The energy sector faces regulatory scrutiny and potential price controls. Stock price as of mid-2024: approximately 25,000-26,500 VND per share.
